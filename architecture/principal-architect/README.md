Welcome to the **Principal AI Infrastructure Architect Learning Repository**! This executive-level curriculum prepares architects for company-wide technical leadership, strategic architecture, and industry thought leadership.

## ✨ What's new — 2026-05-27

The `01-overview.md` lecture for every module (`mod-601-org-wide-architecture` through `mod-605-tech-debt-modernization`) has been expanded from a 5-line stub into a substantive 120-150 line lecture. Each covers the principal-architect altitude, the shift to designing-conditions-for-architecture rather than systems directly, the political layer, and what good looks like. Audit score: 69.

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

By completing this curriculum, you will be able to:

**Enterprise Architecture:**
- Design company-wide AI/ML platform strategies
- Create multi-year technical roadmaps and architecture visions
- Establish architecture governance frameworks
- Lead cross-organizational architecture alignment
- Evaluate and drive emerging technology adoption

**Strategic Leadership:**
- Influence C-level technology decisions
- Present architecture strategy to board of directors
- Align technical vision with business objectives
- Drive M&A technical due diligence
- Shape long-term (3-5 year) technical strategy

**Industry Leadership:**
- Publish architecture thought leadership (conferences, papers)
- Contribute to industry standards and best practices
- Build strategic vendor and partner relationships
- Mentor senior architects across the organization
- Drive innovation and R&D direction

**Organization Impact:**
- Lead architecture review boards (ARB)
- Establish enterprise architecture frameworks (TOGAF, Zachman)
- Drive total cost of ownership (TCO) optimization
- Manage architecture standards and compliance
- Foster architecture excellence culture

## 📝 Project Highlights

### Project 01: Enterprise AI Platform Architecture (80 hours)

Design a company-wide AI/ML platform including:
- Current state assessment (100+ teams)
- Future state architecture vision (3-5 years)
- Migration strategy and roadmap
- Governance and operating model
- TCO analysis and business case

**Deliverables:** Architecture vision document, C-level presentation, migration plan

### Project 02: 3-Year Technology Roadmap (60 hours)

Create strategic technology roadmap including:
- Technology landscape analysis
- Emerging tech evaluation (quantum, edge AI, etc.)
- Platform evolution strategy
- Investment priorities and R&D focus
- Risk assessment and mitigation

**Deliverables:** Technology roadmap, investment proposal, exec briefing

### Project 03: Architecture Governance Framework (60 hours)

Establish enterprise architecture governance:
- Architecture Review Board (ARB) charter
- Design review processes and templates
- Architecture standards and principles
- Compliance and audit framework
- Training and enablement program

**Deliverables:** Governance framework, ARB playbook, standards documentation

### Project 04: M&A Integration Architecture (60 hours)

Lead M&A technical integration:
- Technical due diligence assessment
- Integration architecture design
- Migration and consolidation strategy
- Risk mitigation and contingency planning
- Post-merger architecture roadmap

**Deliverables:** Due diligence report, integration architecture, migration plan

### Project 05: Thought Leadership Portfolio (60 hours)

Demonstrate industry leadership through:
- Conference presentation or paper submission
- Blog series or technical whitepaper
- Open source contribution or framework
- Industry standards contribution
- Patent application (optional)

**Deliverables:** Published work, presentation recordings, portfolio

# Executive Reading List - Senior AI Infrastructure Architect

## Essential Foundation (Must Read)

### Enterprise Architecture

1. **"Enterprise Architecture at Work" - Marc Lankhorst**
   - TOGAF and ArchiMate frameworks
   - Modeling enterprise architecture
   - Strategic alignment

2. **"The Art of Business Value" - Mark Schwartz**
   - Connecting technology to business value
   - Beyond cost-center thinking
   - IT leadership philosophy

3. **"Software Architecture in Practice" - Bass, Clements, Kazman**
   - Architecture fundamentals
   - Quality attributes
   - Design decisions

### Business Strategy

4. **"Good Strategy/Bad Strategy" - Richard Rumelt**
   - What constitutes good strategy
   - Strategic kernel framework
   - Common strategy pitfalls

5. **"The Innovator's Dilemma" - Clayton Christensen**
   - Disruptive innovation
   - Technology adoption curves
   - Strategic responses to disruption

6. **"Blue Ocean Strategy" - Kim & Mauborgne**
   - Value innovation framework
   - Creating uncontested market space
   - Strategic differentiation

### AI and Technology Strategy

7. **"The AI-First Company" - Ash Fontana**
   - Building AI-native businesses
   - Data network effects
   - Competitive moats with AI

8. **"Prediction Machines" - Ajay Agrawal, Joshua Gans, Avi Goldfarb**
   - Economics of AI
   - Strategic implications
   - Decision-making frameworks

### Systems Thinking

9. **"Thinking in Systems" - Donella Meadows**
   - Systems dynamics
   - Leverage points
   - Unintended consequences

10. **"The Phoenix Project" & "The Unicorn Project" - Gene Kim**
    - DevOps transformation
    - IT organizational change
    - Theory of Constraints

### Leadership and Communication

11. **"Made to Stick" - Chip & Dan Heath**
    - Sticky ideas framework (SUCCESs)
    - Communication strategies
    - Memorable messaging

12. **"The Pyramid Principle" - Barbara Minto**
    - Executive communication
    - Structured thinking
    - Persuasive writing

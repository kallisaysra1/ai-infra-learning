# Quiz — strategic partnerships

This module's quiz takes the form of strategic-thinking questions. Discuss
with a peer or mentor. There aren't crisp 'right answers' — defend your
position.

1. When would you de-prioritize strategic partnerships investment for another
   strategic initiative? What's the trade-off?
2. Name three failure modes of a strategic partnerships program and how to detect them early.
3. How would you measure success of a strategic partnerships initiative in 12 months?
4. What's the smallest, lowest-risk first step you could take this quarter?
5. Who are the 3 most important stakeholders to align before launch?

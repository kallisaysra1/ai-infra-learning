# Exercise 05: Board-Level Presentation

Prepare a 10-slide deck on strategic partnerships for a board of directors meeting.
Constraints:
- Slide 1: TL;DR
- Slides 2-7: situation, choices, recommendation
- Slide 8: investment ask
- Slide 9: risks
- Slide 10: discussion / next-step ask

Have a peer review using the 'so what?' test at every slide.

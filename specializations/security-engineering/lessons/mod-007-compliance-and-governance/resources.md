## Things deliberately not on this list

- Vendor compliance whitepapers.
- "Compliance simplified" articles that reduce complex
  regulations to checklists. Always go to the source.
- US legal interpretations of GDPR by non-EU lawyers. Verify EU
  matters with EU counsel.

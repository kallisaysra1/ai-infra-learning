# Exercise 01: Customer Discovery Interviews

Interview 3 users of an ML platform (real or hypothetical). For each:
- 30 min interview
- Identify top 3 frustrations
- Identify top 3 unmet needs
- Distinguish "feature gap" from "platform-knowledge gap"

Synthesize across the 3 into a 1-page report ranking the top 5 platform investments by user pain.

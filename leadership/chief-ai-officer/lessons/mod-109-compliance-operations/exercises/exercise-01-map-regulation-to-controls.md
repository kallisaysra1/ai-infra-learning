# Exercise 01 — Map a Regulation to Controls and Evidence

**Estimated time**: 3 hours
**Deliverable**: A control mapping table for one regulation (≤ 3 pages)

---

## The scenario

You are the CAO at **Tessera Bank**. The state
regulator has begun examining banks' compliance with
EU AI Act Art. 9 (Risk Management System) for their
EU-affecting operations. Tessera has EU-resident
customers via its corporate-banking arm. The CRO has
asked you to **map EU AI Act Art. 9 to Tessera's
specific controls** so that the next examination
finds the program operating against the article.

## Your assignment

Produce a control mapping table that addresses each
of Art. 9's four sub-paragraphs (Art. 9(2)(a)
identification of risks; (b) estimation and
evaluation; (c) other risks from Art. 10 data; (d)
adoption of risk-management measures). For each
sub-paragraph, specify:

- **The obligation** (cite the article text or
  paraphrase the specific requirement).
- **Tessera's control** (the existing or proposed
  operating activity).
- **Evidence produced** (concrete artifacts).
- **Owner** (named role at Tessera).
- **Cadence** (operating cadence).
- **Test** (how the control is verified to operate).

Per §2.3 of the lecture notes, identify
**crosswalks** — other obligations (NIST AI RMF,
ISO 42001, SR 11-7) the same control satisfies.

## Constraints

- Each control must include all six elements from
  §2.1. Partial controls are not acceptable.
- The control map must address all four sub-
  paragraphs of Art. 9(2).
- At least two controls must explicitly
  cross-reference other obligations.
- The control map must use Tessera-specific roles
  (CAO, AI Risk Lead, MRM, etc.), not generic
  "the appropriate function".
- The cadence must be specific (weekly, monthly,
  per-event), not "as needed".

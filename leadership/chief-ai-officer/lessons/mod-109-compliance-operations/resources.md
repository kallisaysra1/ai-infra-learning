# Module 109 — Resources

Annotated reading list for Compliance Operations.

## Tier 1 — Authoritative

| Source | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| [ISO/IEC 42001:2023 Annex A](https://www.iso.org/standard/81230.html) | The control catalog §5 builds on |
| [NIST AI RMF Playbook (GOVERN + MANAGE sub-functions)](https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework) | Crosswalk source for §2.3 |
| [EU AI Act Art. 17 (Quality Management System)](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2024/1689/oj) | The operational obligation underlying continuous practice |
| [ISO/IEC 23894:2023](https://www.iso.org/standard/77304.html) | AI risk management guidance that complements Annex A |

## Tier 2 — Authoritative, sector-specific

| Source | Sector | Use |
|---|---|---|
| [OCC/FRB SR 11-7](https://www.federalreserve.gov/supervisionreg/srletters/sr1107.htm) | Financial services | Model risk controls overlay |
| [NYDFS Part 500](https://www.dfs.ny.gov/industry_guidance/cybersecurity) | NY financial services | Cybersecurity + AI overlay |
| [FDA SaMD documentation guidance](https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices/software-medical-device-samd/) | Healthcare | Medical-device-specific controls |
| [NAIC Model Law on AI Systems](https://content.naic.org/) | Insurance | State-adopted insurance AI controls |
| [SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria](https://www.aicpa-cima.com/topic/audit-assurance/audit-and-assurance-greater-than-soc-2) | Cross-sector | Common framework for evidence-based audit |

# Module 103 — Resources

Annotated reading list for AI Risk Frameworks.

## Tier 1 — Authoritative (read these)

| Source | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| [NIST AI RMF 1.0](https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework) + **the Playbook** | The framework body is short; the Playbook is operationally rich. §3–§5 of the lecture notes reference specific sub-functions. Read the Playbook entries for sub-functions named in the lecture notes. |
| [ISO/IEC 23894:2023](https://www.iso.org/standard/77304.html) — AI Risk Management Guidance | Complements ISO 42001 with AI-specific risk-management vocabulary. Read §6 (Risk assessment) and §7 (Risk treatment) before Exercise 04. |
| [ISO 31000:2018](https://www.iso.org/standard/65694.html) — Risk Management Guidelines | The generic risk-management standard ISO 23894 sits on. Useful for ERM-roll-up framings. |

## Tier 2 — Authoritative, sector-specific

| Source | Use |
|---|---|
| [NIST AI 600-1](https://nvlpubs.nist.gov/nistpubs/ai/NIST.AI.600-1.pdf) — Generative AI Profile | Generative-AI-specific risk vocabulary; useful for any taxonomy that includes generative systems |
| [EU AI Act Art. 9](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2024/1689/oj) | The high-risk Risk Management System obligation; required reading for Exercise 02 |
| [OCC/FRB SR 11-7](https://www.federalreserve.gov/supervisionreg/srletters/sr1107.htm) | The risk-treatment + residual-risk discipline applies broadly even outside financial services |

## Tier 3 — Foundational

| Source | What it gives this module |
|---|---|
| [COSO ERM — Applying ERM to AI (2024)](https://www.coso.org/Pages/erm.aspx) | Bridges AI risk to enterprise risk; useful for §2.1 (taxonomy reconciles to ERM) |
| [IIA Three Lines Model (2020)](https://www.theiia.org/en/content/position-papers/2020/the-iia-three-lines-model/) | The 3LOD application carries through to GOVERN ownership |

## Tier 4 — Practitioner references

| Source | Pattern illustrated |
|---|---|
| Microsoft Responsible AI Standard v2 — impact-assessment template (public) | One working impact-assessment template; useful as comparison material for Exercise 02 |
| [Anthropic Responsible Scaling Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/news/anthropic-responsible-scaling-policy) | Capability-tier residual-risk discipline; useful for §5.3 |
| [Google SAIF](https://safety.google/cybersecurity-advancements/saif/) | Security-overlay control framing; useful for the security-risk category in any taxonomy |

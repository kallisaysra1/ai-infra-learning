# Exercise 04 — Prepare for an Unexpected Board Ask

**Estimated time**: 2 hours
**Deliverable**: Briefing + response (≤ 2 pages)

---

## The scenario

You are the CAO at **Sentinel Mutual Bank**. The
Board Risk Committee chair calls you on Tuesday
afternoon: at the Thursday meeting, the board
plans to ask you a specific question that wasn't
on the agenda.

The chair has shared the question in advance to
give you time to prepare:

> *"In light of recent industry incidents
> involving AI-driven adverse actions and
> regulatory responses, can the CAO explain
> Sentinel's specific exposure to a similar
> event, and what specifically the program is
> doing to mitigate that exposure?"*

You have 36 hours.

## Your assignment

Produce two artifacts.

### Artifact 1 — Internal briefing (≤ 1 page)

A briefing for your own preparation:

- **The question behind the question.** What is
  the board really asking?
- **The honest assessment of Sentinel's
  exposure.** What is real, what is
  manageable, what is concerning.
- **What you should not say.** True things that
  would not advance the conversation if
  volunteered (per the mod-102 Ex-04
  "things-not-to-say" discipline).
- **The 24-hour follow-up plan.** What you
  commit to delivering after the meeting.

### Artifact 2 — Prepared response (≤ 1 page)

Your prepared response to deliver at the
meeting. Three to five paragraphs:

1. Direct opening — name the specific
   exposure.
2. Specific risks identified.
3. Specific mitigations in place or being
   built.
4. Specific gaps or uncertainties.
5. The closing ask of the board (if any).

## Constraints

- The response must be **specific** — no
  generic language about AI risk.
- The response must include **at least one
  honest concession** about something
  Sentinel is not yet doing or has not
  yet resolved.
- The response must include the §5.2 anti-
  patterns explicitly avoided — no
  overclaiming, no hedging, no education-as-
  answer.
- The "what you should not say" section
  must be substantive — not just trivial
  facts.
- The response must be **deliverable in 5
  minutes** at the meeting; not a 20-minute
  educational presentation.

## Rubric

| Criterion | Weight |
|---|---|
| Question-behind-the-question identified | 20% |
| Honest exposure assessment | 25% |
| Specific risks named | 15% |
| Specific mitigations named | 15% |
| Honest concession included | 10% |
| Response is deliverable in 5 minutes | 10% |
| Anti-patterns avoided | 5% |

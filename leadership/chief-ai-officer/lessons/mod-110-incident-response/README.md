# Module 110 — Incident Response

> Module 110 of the Chief AI Officer track. The
> operational treatment of AI incidents across all
> classes from mod-107 §6 — including the EU AI Act
> Art. 73 timelines, sector-specific notification
> obligations, and the post-incident review discipline
> that turns incidents into program improvement.

## What you will leave with

After working through this module you should be able to:

1. Distinguish AI incident response from classical IR
   — and operate the differences without losing
   the discipline classical IR provides.
2. Run the first hour of an AI incident credibly,
   including detection, containment decisions, and
   classification.
3. Apply the notification matrix to specific
   incidents — what gets notified, to whom, when,
   by whom.
4. Lead investigation and root-cause analysis that
   produces program improvement rather than blame.
5. Author post-incident reviews that survive
   external scrutiny and feed the GOVERN loop.
6. Specify a tabletop exercise that tests the
   program's incident response without over-
   investing in performance art.

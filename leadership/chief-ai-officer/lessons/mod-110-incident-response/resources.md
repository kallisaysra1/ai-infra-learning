# Module 110 — Resources

| Source | Why it matters |
|---|---|

| [EU AI Act Art. 73 + Implementing Acts](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2024/1689/oj) | AI-specific notification regime |

| [EU NIS2 Directive](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/dir/2022/2555/oj) | EU cybersecurity incident regime |

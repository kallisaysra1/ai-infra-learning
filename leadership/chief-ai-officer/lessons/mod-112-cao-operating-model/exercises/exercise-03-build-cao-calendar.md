# Exercise 03 — Build a CAO Calendar

**Estimated time**: 2 hours
**Deliverable**: Quarterly + annual calendar (≤ 2 pages)

---

## The scenario

You are the CAO at any of the fictional
institutions used in the track. The Board Risk
Committee chair has casually noted that your
calendar appears entirely reactive. You decide
to author your own quarterly + annual operating
calendar, the one §5.1 describes — deliberately,
not reactively.

## Your assignment

Produce two artifacts.

### Artifact 1 — The recurring rhythm (≤ 1 page)

Per §5.1, the calendar across cadences:

| Cadence | Day / time | Activity | Owner | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Daily | 09:00, 15 min | Standup with AI Risk Lead | AI Risk Lead | Review incidents, escalations |
| Weekly | Monday 09:30 | Weekly bias monitoring review | AI Risk Lead | Operating cadence |
| ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |

Aim for ~15 recurring items across daily, weekly,
bi-weekly, monthly, quarterly, annually.

### Artifact 2 — Time protection (≤ 1 page)

Per §5.2, the protected time blocks:

- **Strategic thinking** — when, how often.
- **Direct report development** — when, how
  often.
- **Stakeholder relationship-building** — when,
  with whom, how often.
- **External engagement** — when, with whom,
  how often.

For each, specify:

- The block.
- The discipline of protection (what
  conditions allow interrupting; what don't).
- The recovery pattern after disruption.

Plus a **crisis discipline statement** per §5.4
— what happens to the calendar during an
incident and how it gets restored.

## Constraints

- The recurring rhythm must include all six §5.1
  cadences (daily through annually).
- At least 3 of the items must be **delegated**
  — the CAO is not the owner per §5.3.
- The time protection must include at least one
  **inviolable** block (no exceptions short of
  catastrophic incident).
- The crisis discipline must include a specific
  **reset pattern** with timing.

## Rubric

| Criterion | Weight |
|---|---|
| All six cadences in recurring rhythm | 25% |
| At least 3 items delegated per §5.3 | 15% |
| Time protection — four areas covered | 25% |
| At least one inviolable block | 10% |
| Crisis discipline with reset pattern | 15% |
| Length discipline — ≤ 2 pages | 10% |

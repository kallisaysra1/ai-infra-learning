# Exercise 01 — Design a Year-1 Operating Plan

**Estimated time**: 3 hours
**Deliverable**: Year-1 operating plan (≤ 4 pages)

---

## The scenario

You are the newly-appointed CAO at **Northfield
Mutual** (continuity from mod-101 Ex-02). Your
appointment was triggered by the state insurance
regulator's inquiry about AI governance posture.
You have 12 months. You need to produce an
operating plan to walk into your first AI Risk
Council meeting.

## Your assignment

Produce a year-1 operating plan with:

### Section 1 — Operating model (≤ ½ page)

The structural choice (per §1) — centralised,
federated, or hub-and-spoke — with reasoning.
For Northfield's mid-market US P&C insurer
profile.

### Section 2 — Quarter-by-quarter milestones (≤ 1½ pages)

Per §2.1, the six things year 1 should
accomplish, distributed across Q1-Q4 with
specific milestones per quarter.

| Quarter | Milestone | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| Q1 | ... | ... |
| Q2 | ... | ... |
| Q3 | ... | ... |
| Q4 | ... | ... |

### Section 3 — Hiring sequence (≤ ½ page)

The 4-5 roles to hire in year 1, in sequence,
with timing per §6.2.

### Section 4 — What year 1 explicitly will not do (≤ ½ page)

Per §2.2 — things year 1 will defer to year 2
with reasoning.

### Section 5 — Mid-year-1 inflection point preparation (≤ ¼ page)

How you will handle the §2.3 inflection point
when the foundation work feels invisible.

### Section 6 — Year-1 self-assessment criteria (≤ ¼ page)

What "year 1 succeeded" means specifically. The
criteria you commit to upfront.

### Section 7 — Budget posture (≤ ½ page)

Year-1 budget framing per §6.3 — tied to risk
reduction, phased, with the cost of not
investing.

## Constraints

- The operating-model choice must be **defended**
  per §1.2 factors.
- Quarterly milestones must be **specific** —
  not "set up the program" but "AI Risk Council
  charter adopted; first meeting held".
- The "what year 1 will not do" section must
  include **at least three** specific things —
  programs that try to do everything in year 1
  fail predictably.
- The hiring sequence must respect §6.2 — the
  AI Risk Lead is hire #1; the other foundation
  roles follow.

## Rubric

| Criterion | Weight |
|---|---|
| Operating model — defended | 15% |
| Quarterly milestones — specific, phased | 30% |
| Hiring sequence per §6.2 | 15% |
| What year 1 will not do — at least three | 15% |
| Inflection point preparation | 10% |
| Self-assessment criteria | 10% |
| Budget posture | 5% |

# Module 112 — The CAO Operating Model

> Closing module of the Chief AI Officer
> foundational track. Ties together the operating
> model from mod-101, the structural choices made
> across mod-104/106/107/108/109, and the
> board-facing discipline from mod-111 — into a
> coherent view of *what running the function
> actually looks like* over a multi-year horizon.

## What you will leave with

After working through this module you should be able to:

1. Make the structural operating-model choices a
   CAO function rests on — centralised vs
   federated vs hub-and-spoke — with awareness of
   the consequences each choice carries forward.
2. Author a year-1 operating plan that survives
   the realities of standing up a new function.
3. Identify what year 2 should look like and how
   the program transitions from foundation-laying
   to consolidation.
4. Recognise what year-3 maturity looks like —
   and the warning signs that the program is
   stalling at year-2.
5. Operate the CAO calendar deliberately rather
   than reactively, including the practice
   patterns that separate effective CAOs from
   exhausted ones.
6. Build a talent and succession strategy that
   doesn't leave the program dependent on a
   single load-bearing executive.

## A note on this being the closing module

The other 11 modules each owned a specific
domain. This module owns the *integration* — how
the domains fit together in practice. It is also
the most personal module: how the CAO operates
day-to-day, week-to-week, year-to-year is
shaped by personal choices alongside structural
ones. The voice acknowledges this; the
exercises don't pretend there's one right answer.

# Capstone 304 — LLM Data-Leak Incident Response

30-day arc. You are CAO at Northrise AI
(the SaaS provider from Capstone 301). A
customer-deployed instance of Northrise's
flagship LLM agent product, GuardianGPT,
has surfaced a serious incident: under
adversarial prompting, the system disclosed
data fragments that appear to originate from
*other customers' tenants*. A handful of
customers have noticed and have begun raising
concerns. The press has not picked it up
yet.

You and the CEO have agreed that you lead
the response. The full team — CISO, General
Counsel, Head of Engineering, Head of
Customer Success, Comms — is at your
disposal.

## Your job

Lead the 30-day incident response: scope
the incident; contain; investigate; notify;
remediate; report internally and externally;
write the post-mortem; close.

# Deliverable 02 — AI/ML Model Inventory

**Target:** Days 10-30. **Length:** Working
inventory + 2-page explanatory memo.

## What this deliverable is

The authoritative AI/ML model inventory for
Tessera, with the in/out determination per
Deliverable 01 already applied. The inventory
itself is an operational artifact (typically
maintained in a GRC tool); the explanatory
memo accompanies it for the Risk Committee.

## What the inventory should contain

Per-model fields:

- Model name and ID
- Business line / shared service
- Use case description
- AI/ML technique (statistical, classical ML,
  deep learning, fine-tuned LLM, vendor LLM,
  RPA-with-ML)
- Whether the model is vendor-supplied or
  internally developed
- In-scope per the policy: yes / no / pending
- Tier: 1 / 2 / 3 (per Deliverable 03;
  pending if not yet tiered)
- Status: in development / in production /
  retired
- Owner (business-line accountable executive)
- Validator (MRM-assigned or pending)
- Last validation date / next validation due
- Material findings open: yes / no
- Notes (red flags, special considerations)

## What the memo should contain

1. **Summary of the inventory.** How many
   models. What proportions across business
   lines. What proportions across techniques.
2. **The four findings from the inventory
   work.** Including the Treasury "smart
   cash position" forecasting tool that has
   been driving real decisions without
   governance. Including the wealth
   management rebalancing model retrained
   without comparison. Including the HR
   vendor LLM. Including any others you
   discover.
3. **Discovery methodology.** How you found
   the models. What you may have missed.
4. **The 28-vs-14 sequencing.** Which of the
   28 outside-MRM models are first into
   tiering / validation work.
5. **Resourcing implications.** What this
   inventory implies for MRM team capacity.

## Constraints

- The Treasury finding is uncomfortable for
  the CRO (it was on her side of the house).
  Naming it honestly is part of the work.
- The HR vendor LLM determination must be
  made and recorded; cannot be left
  "pending" indefinitely.
- The wealth management retrain is an
  active issue that needs attention this
  quarter, not "after MRM is built".

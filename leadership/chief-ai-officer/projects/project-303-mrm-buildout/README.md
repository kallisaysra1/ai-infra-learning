# Capstone 303 — Build an MRM Function for AI/ML at a Regional Bank

90-day arc. You are CAO at Tessera Bank
($25B AUM, US regional, federally regulated).
Tessera has a mature traditional-model MRM
function (credit, market, ops; SR 11-7 aligned)
under the Chief Risk Officer. AI/ML models are
proliferating outside MRM scope. You and the
CRO have agreed to extend MRM to cover AI/ML
within 90 days, in time for the Q4 board
review and the federal banking exam in
February.

## Your job

Define the operating model and produce the
foundational artifacts to put AI/ML under MRM.
The CRO owns the MRM function; you own the
AI-specific extensions. The split-authorship
boundary from mod-104 is the working model.

# Deliverable 01 — Classification Analysis

**Target:** Days 1-5. **Length:** ≤ 3 pages.
**Modules:** mod-102 §2.2 (risk tiers); mod-102
Ex-01 (classification practice).

## What this deliverable is

The first artifact: a documented EU AI Act
classification analysis for KH-AI-027. Establishes
the system as high-risk, names the Article 6
basis, and documents the Art. 6(3) exemption
analysis that was considered and rejected.

This is the foundational artifact — every
subsequent deliverable refers to it.

## What it should contain

1. **System description** (≤ ½ page) — what
   the system does, intended use, deployer
   context.
2. **Art. 6(1) analysis** — is the system a
   safety component of a regulated product
   requiring third-party conformity? Yes —
   the underlying medical device (under EU
   MDR Class IIa) requires third-party
   conformity, and the AI system functions as
   a safety component.
3. **Art. 6(2) analysis** — does the system
   fall under Annex III? Yes — Annex III(5)(a)
   "essential private services... healthcare"
   applies as a secondary basis.
4. **Art. 6(3) exemption analysis** — does
   any of the four exemption conditions
   apply (narrow procedural task; improve
   prior human activity; detect deviations
   without replacing assessment; preparatory
   task)? **No.** Document the analysis
   explicitly; the workflow is
   non-ophthalmologist-led screening where
   the AI's suggestion influences (not just
   prepares) the referral decision.
5. **Conformity assessment route** —
   Annex VI internal control OR Annex VII
   third-party assessment? Defend the choice.
6. **EU AI Act database registration plan
   per Art. 49.**

## Constraints

- Cite **specific Article and sub-article
  references** throughout.
- The Art. 6(3) exemption analysis must be
  **substantive** — naming each of the four
  conditions explicitly and reasoning
  whether it applies.
- The conformity route choice must be
  defended on substance, not convenience.

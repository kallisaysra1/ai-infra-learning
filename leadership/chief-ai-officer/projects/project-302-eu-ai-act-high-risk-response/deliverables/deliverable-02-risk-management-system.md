# Deliverable 02 — Article 9 Risk Management System

**Target:** Days 5-10. **Length:** 6-8 pages.
**Modules:** mod-102 §2.3; mod-103 entire; mod-102
Ex-03 (Art. 9 RMS summary).

## What this deliverable is

The full Art. 9 Risk Management System document
for KH-AI-027. Unlike mod-102 Ex-03 (which was a
1-page summary for board use), this is the
**full document** that becomes part of the
Annex IV technical file. Notified Body will
read this in detail.

## What it should contain

Per Art. 9(2)(a) through (d) plus the
iterative-process requirement:

1. **System scope and classification.**
2. **Art. 9(2)(a) Identification of risks.**
   The taxonomy from mod-103 §2 applied to
   KH-AI-027. At least 8 specific risks
   named with likelihood × severity ratings.
3. **Art. 9(2)(b) Estimation and evaluation
   of risks from intended use and reasonably
   foreseeable misuse.** Estimation
   methodology; evaluation thresholds.
4. **Art. 9(2)(c) Data risks.** Cross-
   reference to Deliverable 03 (Art. 10
   data documentation).
5. **Art. 9(2)(d) Risk management
   measures.** Per-risk treatment plans
   following mod-103 Ex-04 pattern. Residual
   risk explicitly named and accepted.
6. **Continuous, iterative process.**
   Governance machinery; review cadence;
   integration with post-market monitoring
   (cross-reference to Deliverable 06).
7. **Deployer communication** per Art. 13.

## Constraints

- The bias-risk treatment **must address the
  mod-105 §3.2 impossibility result** —
  naming which fairness conception KH-AI-027
  prioritises.
- At least 3 risks must include **residual
  acceptance** by a named role with re-
  evaluation triggers.
- The diabetic-retinopathy-specific clinical
  risks must be addressed substantively —
  subgroup performance for elderly patients,
  patients with cataracts, patients with
  diverse retinal pigmentation.

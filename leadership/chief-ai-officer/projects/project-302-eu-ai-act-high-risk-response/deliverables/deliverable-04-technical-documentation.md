# Deliverable 04 — Art. 11 + Annex IV Technical Documentation Summary

**Target:** Days 18-25. **Length:** 4-5 pages
(summary; full technical file is much longer
and is engineering's deliverable).

## What this deliverable is

The summary section of the Annex IV technical
documentation. The full Annex IV file is
authored by engineering (~50-100 pages); the
CAO produces the **executive summary** that
maps the file's contents against Annex IV's
required content list and serves as the
Notified Body's reading guide.

## What it should contain

Per Annex IV (numbered against the Annex):

1. **General description** — intended purpose;
   provider; versions; performance metrics.
2. **Detailed description of the elements
   of the AI system and the process for its
   development.**
3. **Detailed information about the monitoring,
   functioning, and control of the AI system.**
4. **Description of the appropriateness of
   the performance metrics for the specific
   AI system.**
5. **Risk management system** (cross-reference
   to Deliverable 02).
6. **Lifecycle changes management.**
7. **List of harmonised standards applied** —
   ISO 13485 (medical device quality);
   IEC 62304 (medical device software lifecycle);
   ISO 14971 (medical device risk
   management); ISO 42001 (AI management
   system) — partial; IEC 60601-1 (electrical
   safety for the integrated imaging hardware).
8. **A copy of the EU declaration of
   conformity.** (Drafted; finalized after
   Notified Body sign-off.)

For each Annex IV section, the summary names:

- What document or section in the technical
  file addresses the requirement.
- Page numbers (placeholder; populated as
  the full file is finalised).
- Specific evidence to which the section
  links (e.g., audit ledger references; per
  mod-108 §4).

## Constraints

- The summary must be **navigable** — a
  Notified Body reviewer should be able to
  jump from this summary to the specific
  technical-file content for any Annex IV
  item.
- **Harmonised standards** list must be
  substantive — not just AI-specific but
  including the medical-device standards
  that apply.
- The summary must address the **lifecycle
  changes** dimension explicitly per Art. 14
  (planned changes) and per FDA PCCP-style
  predetermined change control (cross-
  references the FDA work).

## Rubric

| Criterion | Weight |
|---|---|
| All Annex IV sections addressed | 30% |
| Navigability — links to technical file | 20% |
| Harmonised standards substantive | 20% |
| Lifecycle changes management | 15% |
| Cross-references to other deliverables | 10% |
| Length discipline 4-5 pages summary | 5% |

# Capstone 302 — EU AI Act High-Risk Classification Response

> Second capstone of the CAO track. Walks through a
> full EU AI Act conformity response for one
> specific high-risk AI system, integrating
> mod-102, mod-103, mod-105, mod-108, mod-109, and
> mod-110 into the substantive artifacts an EU
> Notified Body, the national competent authority,
> and the firm's own board need to see.

## What this capstone is for

mod-102 introduced the EU AI Act at the level a
working CAO needs to read it. This capstone makes
you *produce the artifacts the Act requires* for
one specific high-risk system. By the end, the
reader has authored a complete EU AI Act
conformity package — not a sample, the actual
package.

## Estimated time

**25–35 hours.** Distributed across multiple
sessions. This is the longest capstone in the
track because the artifact set is the most
extensive.

## What you will produce

Seven substantial deliverables plus a synthesis
memo:

| # | Deliverable | Article(s) |
|---|---|---|
| 01 | Classification analysis + Art. 6(3) exemption analysis | Art. 6 + Annex III |
| 02 | Risk management system (full document) | Art. 9 |
| 03 | Data and data governance documentation | Art. 10 |
| 04 | Technical documentation summary | Art. 11 + Annex IV |
| 05 | Human oversight design | Art. 14 |
| 06 | Post-market monitoring system | Art. 72 |
| 07 | Serious incident reporting procedure | Art. 73 |
| — | Synthesis memo for the conformity submission | — |

## The scenario

You are the CAO at **Kerridge Healthcare**
(continuity from mod-101 Ex-05 Kerridge Industries
+ mod-103 Ex-05 retinal-imaging capstone).
Kerridge Healthcare's diabetic-retinopathy
screening SaMD is approaching EU launch. The
system is in pre-submission state with FDA
(De Novo); EU MDR (Class IIa); and EU AI Act
high-risk. The Notified Body engagement begins in
60 days. The internal kickoff for EU AI Act
conformity is now.

See [`SCENARIO.md`](./SCENARIO.md) for the full
context.

## How to work the capstone

Unlike Capstone 301's 90-day calendar, this
capstone has a *submission deadline* — 60 days
to Notified Body engagement. The deliverables
build toward the submission:

- **Days 1–10:** Deliverable 01 (classification)
  + Deliverable 02 (RMS — the foundational
  artifact).
- **Days 11–25:** Deliverables 03 (data) + 04
  (technical doc).
- **Days 26–40:** Deliverable 05 (human
  oversight) + 06 (PMM).
- **Days 41–50:** Deliverable 07 (incident
  reporting).
- **Days 51–60:** Synthesis memo + internal
  review + Notified Body submission preparation.

## Module integration

This capstone exercises:

- **mod-102** §2 (EU AI Act in depth) — the
  primary anchor.
- **mod-103** §3 (MAP), §4 (MEASURE), §5
  (MANAGE) — the RMS components.
- **mod-105** §3 (bias), §4 (transparency) — the
  ethics dimensions of the artifacts.
- **mod-108** §4 (evidence packages) — the
  Notified Body submission is itself a kind of
  evidence package.
- **mod-109** §2 (control mapping) — Annex IV
  documentation is structured control mapping.
- **mod-110** §6 (incident response) — Art. 73
  procedure operationalizes mod-110 disciplines.

# Deliverable 06 — Compliance Operations Baseline

**Target days:** 61–75 of the 90-day arc.
**Length:** ≤ 3 pages.
**Module integration:** mod-109 §2 (control
mapping) + mod-109 §3 (continuous evidence).

---

## What this deliverable is

The compliance operations baseline. Maps the
regulatory and customer-contractual obligations
Northrise faces onto specific controls, and
establishes the evidence-collection cadence.

This is the document that lets sales teams
answer enterprise customer governance
questions with substance.

## What it should contain

Three sections.

### Section 1 — Obligations register

Per mod-102 §6.1 + mod-109 §2 patterns. The
register identifies Northrise's AI-related
obligations across:

- **EU AI Act** — for Northrise-hosted EU
  operations and customer-deployed agents that
  process EU-resident data.
- **NYDFS Part 500** — for Northrise's
  financial-services customer base.
- **State AI laws** — CA AI Transparency Act,
  CO Privacy Act AI rules, NYC AEDT (if any
  customers use products for hiring).
- **Customer contractual** — SOC 2 + ISO 42001
  + AI BOM commitments to top-20 customers.
- **Sector-specific** — for healthcare
  customers using Northrise products on PHI.

Aim for ~15-20 distinct obligations. Each named
with its regulatory or contractual basis.

### Section 2 — Initial control map

Per mod-109 §2.4 pattern. ~10 controls covering
the major obligation categories. Each control:

- Obligation(s) satisfied.
- Activity.
- Evidence produced.
- Owner (named Northrise role).
- Cadence.
- Test.

Use the mod-109 §2.3 crosswalk pattern — one
control covers multiple obligations where
appropriate.

### Section 3 — Continuous evidence cadence

For one specific control (the AI inventory
control, by recommendation), the full
continuous-evidence cadence per mod-109 Ex-02
pattern.

## Northrise-specific considerations

- **Customer-deployed risk.** Some controls
  apply at customer edge; Northrise must
  influence rather than directly enforce.
- **Existing SOC 2.** Northrise has Type 1;
  Type 2 in progress. Many controls Northrise
  already operates can be cross-referenced.
- **Resource constraints.** Year-1 budget
  doesn't support full operational
  infrastructure. Controls must be
  prioritised to what year 1 can credibly
  operate.

## Discipline applied

- **Control map crosswalks** — one control
  satisfies multiple obligations per mod-109
  §2.3.
- **Specific named owners** per mod-109 Ex-01
  pattern.
- **Evidence cadence** addresses Northrise's
  evidence infrastructure constraints (no
  audit ledger yet; SOC 2 logging exists).

## Rubric

| Criterion | Weight |

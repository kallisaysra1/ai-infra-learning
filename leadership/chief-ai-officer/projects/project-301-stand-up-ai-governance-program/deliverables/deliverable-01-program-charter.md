# Deliverable 01 — Program Charter

**Target days:** 1–10 of the 90-day arc.
**Length:** ≤ 3 pages.
**Module integration:** mod-101 §5 (operating
model); mod-101 §4 (CAO scope).

---

## What this deliverable is

The Northrise AI program charter — the document
that, once approved by the CEO and acknowledged
by the Board, establishes the program's
authority, scope, and operating model.

This is the first artifact you produce. Without
it, every subsequent deliverable rests on
informal authority that will be tested under
pressure.

## What it should contain

Per mod-101 Ex-02 reference solution pattern,
adapted to Northrise's context:

1. **Mission.** One paragraph. What this program
   exists to do.
2. **Scope.** What systems and what activities
   are in scope. What is explicitly out of scope.
3. **Authority.** Decision authority; standard-
   setting authority; recommendation authority;
   escalation process.
4. **Structure.** Where the CAO reports; the
   relationship to peer executives; the
   governance bodies (AI Risk Council, AI Review
   Board).
5. **Standards alignment.** Which authoritative
   framework anchors the program. Justify in
   two sentences.
6. **Risk appetite posture (pending formal
   adoption).** How the program operates while
   the formal appetite statement (Deliverable
   04) is being drafted.
7. **First-90-day deliverables.** This
   capstone's seven deliverables, framed for
   the CEO.

## Discipline applied

- **Operating model choice** explicit. Per
  mod-112 §1, defended for Northrise's
  context.
- **Authority claims** must be traceable to
  the CEO's appointment letter and a Board
  acknowledgment.
- **Charter must be regulator-presentable** —
  written so a peer CAO or a regulator could
  read it without translation.

## Rubric

| Criterion | Weight |
|---|---|
| Mission — one substantive paragraph | 10% |
| Scope — explicit in and out | 15% |
| Authority — make / block / recommend distinguished | 20% |
| Structure — operating model defended; governance bodies named | 20% |
| Standards alignment — anchored to Tier-1 framework with justification | 10% |
| Risk appetite posture pending — defensible | 10% |
| First-90-day deliverables — the seven of this capstone | 10% |
| Length discipline — ≤ 3 pages | 5% |

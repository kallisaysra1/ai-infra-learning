# Deliverable 04 — AI Risk Appetite Statement

**Target days:** 26–45 of the 90-day arc.
**Length:** ≤ 3 pages.
**Module integration:** mod-111 Ex-01 pattern.

---

## What this deliverable is

Northrise's AI risk appetite statement. The
document the Board adopts that says how much AI
risk Northrise is willing to take.

## What it should contain

Per mod-111 Ex-01 reference solution structure:

1. **Preamble.** What AI risk means at
   Northrise; what this statement does.
2. **Risk categories.** Drawn from the
   Deliverable 02 taxonomy.
3. **For each category, the appetite.** Per
   mod-111 §2.4 patterns — risk-class framing,
   boundary language, threshold language.
4. **Escalation process.** What happens when
   a case approaches or exceeds the boundary.
5. **Review cadence.** When the Board
   reconsiders.

## Northrise-specific considerations

- **Customer-deployed risk** category needs
  explicit appetite. What's Northrise's
  posture on agent behaviour at customer
  edge? Is appetite different for Northrise-
  hosted vs. customer-deployed?
- **Series D pressure.** The appetite
  statement will be diligenced by Series D
  investors. Specific quantitative thresholds
  matter for that audience.
- **Mid-market customer base.** Northrise's
  customers are smaller and more numerous than
  Halverston's institutional clients. The
  appetite for incidents-affecting-customers
  must reflect this.

## Discipline applied

- **Board-readable** per mod-111 §2.4.
- **Combines at least two appetite-expression
  patterns** per category.
- **Bias category** acknowledges mod-105
  impossibility result.
- **Customer-deployed risk category** addressed
  explicitly — Northrise's flagship product
  surface.

## Rubric

| Criterion | Weight |
|---|---|
| Structure — five sections per §2.3 | 15% |

# Deliverable 07 — First Quarterly Board Report

**Target days:** 76–90 of the 90-day arc.
**Length:** ≤ 5 pages (main report) + appendices.
**Module integration:** mod-111 Ex-02 pattern.

---

## What this deliverable is

The first quarterly AI program report to the
Northrise Board Risk Committee. This is the
artifact that closes the 90-day arc.

The report covers the program's first
quarter — much of which was the program being
*built*. The structure of mod-111 §3.1 still
applies but the content is necessarily
foundation-progress-heavy rather than
operating-results-heavy.

## What it should contain

Per mod-111 Ex-02 structure:

1. **Risk posture summary.** Northrise's risk
   posture by category from Deliverable 02.
   First-quarter assessments are necessarily
   preliminary.
2. **Material changes.** What the program
   built and operationalized in the quarter —
   the six prior deliverables.
3. **Material exceptions.** Items currently
   outside risk appetite. First-quarter:
   primarily the gap between Northrise's
   current state and the appetite statement
   just adopted.
4. **Material decisions pending.** What the
   Board needs to decide.
5. **Material changes ahead.** Q2 (year 1 Q2)
   focus.
6. **Asks.** At least one specific ask per
   mod-111 §3.2.

Plus appendices as reference material.

## Northrise-specific considerations

- **First-quarter report dynamic.** The Board
  is reading this as a status report on the
  program build, not as ongoing operations.
  The tone is appropriately foundation-
  focused.
- **Series D context.** The report will be
  shown (or excerpts will be) to Series D
  investors during the upcoming raise. Tone
  appropriate for both audiences.
- **CEO relationship.** Per Deliverable 01,
  CAO reports to CEO; the Board engagement is
  through the CEO + Audit Committee.

## Discipline applied

- **Honesty discipline** per mod-111 §3.3.
- **Asks discipline** per mod-111 §3.2 — at
  least one ask.
- **Avoids anti-patterns** per mod-111 §1.3 —
  no operational metrics in detail; no
  technology details.
- **Material exceptions** addressed honestly
  even when the program is in foundation mode.

## Rubric

| Criterion | Weight |

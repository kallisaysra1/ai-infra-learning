# Deliverable 03 — Year-1 Operating Plan

**Target days:** 18–25 of the 90-day arc.
**Length:** ≤ 4 pages.
**Module integration:** mod-112 Ex-01 pattern.

---

## What this deliverable is

The year-1 operating plan for the Northrise AI
program. Follows the mod-112 Ex-01 reference
solution pattern adapted for Northrise's
high-growth SaaS context.

## What it should contain

Per mod-112 Ex-01 structure:

1. **Operating model.** The structural choice
   per mod-101 §5 + mod-112 §1. Defended for
   Northrise.
2. **Quarter-by-quarter milestones.** Q1-Q4
   with specific milestones per quarter.
   *Note: this deliverable is being authored in
   Q1 itself; days 1-90 represent the first
   quarter. Q2-Q4 milestones are forward-
   looking.*
3. **Hiring sequence.** 4-5 roles to hire in
   year 1 against the $X hiring budget.
4. **What year 1 will not do.** Explicit
   defer list per mod-112 §2.2.
5. **Mid-year-1 inflection point preparation.**
   How the inflection point is handled per
   mod-112 §2.3.
6. **Year-1 self-assessment criteria.** What
   "year 1 succeeded" means at Northrise
   specifically.
7. **Budget posture.** Per mod-112 §6.3.

## Northrise-specific considerations

- **Growth velocity.** Northrise is growing
  fast; the year-1 plan must accommodate
  velocity that wouldn't apply at slower
  institutions.
- **Customer-deployed infrastructure.** Some
  governance work happens at customers' edge,
  not Northrise's center. This affects what
  year 1 can directly control vs. what year 1
  must influence.
- **Series D pressure.** The Series D
  conversation in 6 months is a forcing
  function on what must be ready when.

## Discipline applied

- Quarterly milestones **specific** — not
  "set up program" but specific named
  artifacts.
- "What year 1 will not do" — at least three
  specific things deferred.
- Hiring sequence **respects mod-112 §6.2**
  pattern; AI Risk Lead first.
- Budget posture **tied to risk reduction**,
  not abstract program activity.

## Rubric

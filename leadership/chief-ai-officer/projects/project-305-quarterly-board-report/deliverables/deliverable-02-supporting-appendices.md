# Deliverable 02 — Supporting Appendices

**Target length:** As needed; reference
material.

## What this deliverable is

The supporting appendices to the Q2
Board report. Not all directors will
read these; the technically-engaged
directors and the Risk Committee chair
will. They support the report's
substance without bloating the main
document.

## Suggested appendices

### A — AI/ML model inventory snapshot

Quantitative view of the 78 models:
distribution by business line,
technique, tier, status. Year-over-
year and quarter-over-quarter
change. Visualised where useful.

### B — Top risks deep-dive

For each top risk named in Section 4
of the main report: substantive
description, trend data, mitigation
status, residual position.

### C — Q2 incident summary

For each Q2 event:
- Hallucination in the LLM agent
  research-support tool.
- Alpha-generation signal pause and
  redeploy.
- Vendor LLM upstream incident.
- EU AI Act high-risk classification
  surfacing.
- CFPB inquiry.
- MRM hiring outcomes.

Each: full timeline; what was done;
status; lessons.

### D — Regulatory horizon detail

Per-jurisdiction detail on AI
regulatory developments that affect
Halverston:
- EU AI Act high-risk classification
  for Halverston systems.
- SEC AI rulemaking watch.
- CFPB AI guidance.
- State-level rulemaking (NY,
  California, Colorado).
- Asia-pacific (MAS, Hong Kong SFC).
- UK (FCA).

### E — MRM validation pipeline status

Current state of the validation
backlog; per-model status; capacity
analysis; projections.

### F — Ongoing monitoring scorecard

Stream-by-stream monitoring data for
the quarter. Drift indicators,
performance trends, subgroup
performance status.

### G — AI Acceptable Use Policy
adherence metrics

Compliance with the AAUP across
business lines; training completion
rates; reported violations and
disposition.

### H — Vendor AI risk register

Halverston's vendor AI exposure.
Vendor list, services, risk
assessments, monitoring status.

### I — Forward-looking horizon — 24
months

The 24-month horizon for AI at
Halverston: regulatory, product,
infrastructure, organisational.
Supports the CAO's Section 7
"what we are worrying about"
framing.

## Constraints

- Appendices are reference, not
  narrative. Each appendix is
  self-contained.
- Format supports skim by
  technically-engaged directors.
- Updates quarter-over-quarter
  trackable; template-quality.

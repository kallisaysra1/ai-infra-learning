# Capstone 305 — Quarterly Board AI Risk Report

Single-deliverable capstone. You are CAO at
Halverston Capital, a $40B asset manager.
You have been CAO for 18 months. The Q2
board meeting is in 2 weeks. The Risk
Committee has asked you to prepare and
present a comprehensive quarterly AI risk
report — the third such report you've
produced, but the first one the full board
(not just Risk Committee) will review,
following Halverston's recent decision to
elevate AI risk to board-level standing
governance.

## Your job

Write the Q2 quarterly AI risk report for
the board. Build it cleanly. This report
sets the template for the standing board
review going forward — it's both a
quarterly deliverable and a precedent.

## Deliverables

```
project-305-quarterly-board-report/
├── README.md
├── SCENARIO.md
└── deliverables/
    ├── deliverable-01-board-report.md
    └── deliverable-02-supporting-appendices.md
```

Just two deliverables this time. The
board report itself is the substantive
artifact; the appendices support it. This
capstone tests synthesis and audience-
discipline rather than breadth.
